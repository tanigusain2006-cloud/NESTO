NESTO

Neurodiverse Education Support Tool

A calm space for curious minds

NESTO helps children explore, learn, and grow through gentle, activity-based experiences. No pressure. No scores. Just thoughtful play at their own pace.

Explore Activities For Parents

Important to Know

NESTO is not a medical, diagnostic, or therapeutic tool. It's simply a collection of gentle, engaging activities designed to support exploration and comfort. Always consult qualified professionals for specific developmental or health concerns.

Understanding NESTO

What is NESTO?

A thoughtfully designed platform where every child can explore activities that feel comfortable, predictable, and engaging.

Child-First Design

Every element is created with young learners in mind. Big buttons, clear visuals, and calm colors make navigation intuitive and stress-free.

Activity-Based Learning

Real-world interactions like matching, tracing, and exploring. Each activity supports natural curiosity without artificial challenges.

Neurodiverse Support

Built with understanding of diverse learning needs. Predictable patterns, sensory-friendly design, and no unexpected surprises.

Our Approach

Why NESTO is Different

We believe learning should feel like play, not pressure. NESTO removes the elements that cause stress and keeps what sparks joy.

No Scores or Rankings

Every interaction is valuable. No points, no levels, no comparisons.

No Timers or Pressure

Children move at their own pace. Activities wait patiently.

No Ads Ever

A completely ad-free experience. No interruptions, no distractions.

Smooth Transitions

Gentle animations guide between activities. No jarring changes.

Your Child's Safety

Privacy & Safety

We take your child's privacy seriously. Here's our commitment to you.

Safe Environment

NESTO is a closed, protected space with no external links, no social features, and no communication with strangers.

Data Protection

All data is encrypted and stored securely. We collect only what's necessary for the experience and nothing more.

Parent Control

Parents have full control over their child's account, activities, and data. You can export or delete everything at any time.
